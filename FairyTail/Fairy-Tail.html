Fairy Tail (フェアリーテイル)

Brief Introduction of the Story

In a world full of magic - "Earth Land", there is a Guild located in The Kingdom of Fiore, many powerful sorcerers gathered among them - it's the Guild of the "Fairy Tail".
Lucy Heartfilia, the psychic sorcerer, has always wanted to become a member of this Guild. Under the trouble taking by Natsu the guidance of Natsu who is one of the members of "Fairy Tail" nicknamed "Salamander", Lucy finally gets her wish and gets to know many powerful sorcerers. Lucy then joins Natsu, Gray, Erza and Happy as the "The Most Destructive Strongest Team" to create countless legends in the world's noisiest, most violent, but also the happiest guilds. They grow stronger and stronger in order to fulfill the tasks of various clients, their partners join the guild one by one, and the story begins to unravel...



Main Characters

Lucy Heartfilia

One of the Fairy Tail's strongest squad members.
Uses astral magic, an astral sorcerer, daughter of the Heartfilia group, the most normal girl in the Fairy Tail, and one of the most popular sorcerer in this guild. Lucy gets along very well with everyone because of her lively, outgoing and partner-oriented personality.

  • "What I need is neither money nor nice clothes. It is a place that is willing to accept the "I" as a person. The Fairy Tail is my other home...A home much warmer than this."

Gray Fullbuster

One of the Fairy Tail's strongest squad members.
Uses the magic of ice, the ice form sorcerer, who inherits his father Silver's magic to destroy evil and becomes the ice sorcerer of Devilslayer, the apprentice of the ice sorcerer uru, who can make ice into various shapes to use as a weapon. And contrary to his magic, he was warm and generous. But he has a tendency to take off his clothes quickly.

Happy

One of the Fairy Tail's strongest squad members, the Exceed.
Using winged magic, Happy was born from an egg Natsu found and has been stuck to Natsu ever since. They always working together and living together. Although he's not capable of fighting, Naysu often use Happy's winged magic to help him chase enemies or fight in the air during combat. Happy is also able to give Natsu some advice in battle, making him an indispensable partner in both life and battle.
