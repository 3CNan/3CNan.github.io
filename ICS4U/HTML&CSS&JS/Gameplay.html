Blackjack

Blackjack(twenty-one/Pontoon)

- One of the most popular casino card games in the world


Hit: Take another card.
Stand: Take no more cards.
Double down: Double the wager, take exactly one more card, and then stand.


Players try to get the maximum point equal or less than 21 points.

It's able to calculate the probability of the point on the next card because the game will use a certain amount of decks of cards until finished.

Pick One

Pick One(Old Maid)

- An old game since 19 centuries.


  1. Remove a card from the decks, which can be a Joker or any cards.
  2. The dealer shuffles the deck and deals all the cards to the players, one card at a time.
  3. Players look at their cards and discard any pair of them.
    The game continues with players taking cards and discarding pairs until there are no more pairs.
  4. The player with an unmatched card loses.

Fishing Kitten

Fishing Kitten

- A simple game for everyone who's struggling on poker rules.


  1. The dealer shuffles the deck and deals all the cards to the players, one card at a time.
  2. Players turn over the top card at a time and place it in the queue.
  3. Each time a card is played, if there is a card with the same number, that player may take all the cards between those two cards and place them under their deck.
    The game continues with players taking cards and discarding pairs until there are no more pairs.
  4. Players play cards by turn, and the only player with cards left wins.
