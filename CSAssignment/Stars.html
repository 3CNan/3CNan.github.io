In 2015, they released their first album-"Oh Wonder",contains songs "Drive","Without You", and "Plans" total 15 songs.There were over 1 millions ablums were sold around the world after the albums were released. Then, in 2017, they released second album "Ultralife." The album sales were still impressive. Most people like their styles because it's relax and soft. Josephine's sweet voice really attract lots of people. If you begin interested in their songs, you can coutinue to find in the rank list.

"HYUKOH" is a South Korean band. The band was formed in 2014. It include singer and guitarist Oh Hyuk,bassist Im Dong-geon, guitarist Lim Hyun-jae, and drummer Lee In-woo. Their first mini album released in September in 2014. All the songs in the album are in English. The album didn't have any propanganda,but HYUKOH began to be known in the grand underground. The style of their band is relax and psychedelic. Especially singer Oh Hyuk's lazy and magnetic voice add more beautiful color to this band.
