About Us

Our Aim: World's No.1 Game Platform

INTRO

OPENGAME is a cutting-edge tech company in the gaming industry, focusing on developing and globally distributing mobile games. Notable titles include "Merge Watermelon," "Save the Princess," and "Ocean Crush," with over 100 million downloads worldwide.

MANAGEMENT

OPENGAME uses a flat management structure, values transparent communication, and offers competitive benefits like comprehensive insurance, performance and project bonuses, and equity incentives.

PARTNERSHIP

As an international gaming firm, OPENGAME partners with Google, Apple, Facebook, and TikTok. The team includes experts from top companies like Baidu, Alibaba, and Tencent, with vast experience in mobile game development and operations.

FUTURE

The company promotes continuous learning and growth, organizes team-building activities, and offers lifestyle benefits. With ongoing performance growth, OPENGAME aims to enhance technological innovation and platform operations.

Join Us

Proactive and Responsible

Game Planner

Job Responsibilities:
  • Oversee requirements, design, development, testing, growth, and analysis.
  • Communicate with departments, coordinate resources, and ensure project progress.
  • Analyze data, track feedback, and improve retention and conversion.
Job Requirements:
  • Proficient in software for product requirements, flowcharts, and mind maps.
  • Skilled in funnel models and user segmentation.
  • Passionate about games, knowledgeable about game types, and internet product logic.

Data Analyst

Job Responsibilities:
  • Analyze game data: user behavior, revenue, retention.
  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to improve user experience.
  • Develop and maintain data models and reports.
Job Requirements:
  • Proficient in SQL, Python, R, Tableau; experience with Hadoop, Spark is a plus.
  • Ability to use data for product decisions and explain results clearly.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills, thrive in fast-paced environments.

Game UI Designer

Job Responsibilities:
  • Design intuitive and attractive game UI elements based on game style and player needs.
  • Create prototypes and final UI elements, including icons, buttons, and panels.
  • Stay updated on current game UI design trends and best practices.
Job Requirements:
  • Good visual aesthetics and sensitivity to color, layout, and typography.
  • Basic understanding of game UI design principles. Strong communication skills and team spirit.
  • Experience with Unity or other game engines is a plus.
