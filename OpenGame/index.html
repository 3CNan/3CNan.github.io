About Us

Our Aim: World's No.1 Game Platform

Leading in Entertainment & Revenue

INTRO

OPENGAME is a cutting-edge tech company in the gaming industry, focusing on developing and globally distributing mobile games. Notable titles include "Merge Watermelon," "Save the Princess," and "Ocean Crush," with over 100 million downloads worldwide.

MANAGEMENT

OPENGAME uses a flat management structure, values transparent communication, and offers competitive benefits like comprehensive insurance, performance and project bonuses, and equity incentives.

PARTNERSHIP

As an international gaming firm, OPENGAME partners with Google, Apple, Facebook, and TikTok. The team includes experts from top companies like Baidu, Alibaba, and Tencent, with vast experience in mobile game development and operations.

FUTURE

The company promotes continuous learning and growth, organizes team-building activities, and offers lifestyle benefits. With ongoing performance growth, OPENGAME aims to enhance technological innovation and platform operations.

Game Designer

Job Responsibilities:
  • Plan the values and level strategies for the company's overseas casual games.
  • Oversee the entire process from product requirements, design style formulation, R&D testing, user growth, to data analysis.
  • Communicate with various departments to coordinate resources and ensure project progress.
  • Analyze product data, track user feedback, and improve product retention and user conversion rates.
  • Monitor industry trends and competitor performance in casual games to optimize and enhance game experience and increase user engagement and playtime.
Job Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree or higher from a recognized institution, with a passing score in CET-4 (425+).
  • Proficient in using software related to product requirements, flowcharts, and mind maps, and capable of independently writing highly readable product requirement documents.
  • Skilled in using funnel models and user segmentation for data analysis.
  • Passionate about games, with an interest in exploring and researching various game types, and understanding the commercial logic of internet products.
  • Strong logical thinking, excellent communication skills, stress resilience, and a strong sense of self-motivation.
