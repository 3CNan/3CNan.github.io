Sword Art Online

Kirito & Asuna

Humans finally achieved full virtual reality in 2022 when ARGUS, an electronic-machinery manufacturer, developed a "NERvGear" headset that could control the nerves in the human brain to connect to the virtual world.
As one of the players in the VRMMORPG (Virtual Reality Massively Mutiplayer Online Role-playing Game) Kirigaya Kazuto and 10,000 other players had just logged in to enjoy the game, the game administrator announced a frightening news to everyone.
That is, the only way to log out of the game is to finish the game, and in this game "GAME OVER" means "death" in the real world.
Kirito could only accept this fact earlier than other players in this chaotic situation, and threw himself into the battle with no end in sight...
