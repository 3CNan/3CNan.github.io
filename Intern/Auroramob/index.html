About Us

Intro
Auroramob is a high-tech company developing tool-based applications, providing efficient and intelligent digital solutions for users worldwide. Our mission is to enhance the quality of daily life and work efficiency through technological innovation.
Products
Auroramob's core products cover three main areas: playkets, translator, and AI picture processing. Each application is meticulously designed to provide users with the best possible experience.
Establishment
Founded in May 2017, it is located in the beautiful spring city of Jinan, a high-tech enterprise in Shandong Province. It is also one of Shandong's top mobile Internet and artificial intelligence companies.
Members
All the company's founding members graduated from 985 and other double first-class universities in China. In the early stage, the core members worked in Baidu, Innovation Workshop, FunPlus, and other world-class Internet and game companies.
Partnerships
Our company has strategic agreements with Tencent, Baidu, ByteDance, and KuaiShou domestically, and with Amazon, Google, Apple, Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok internationally.
