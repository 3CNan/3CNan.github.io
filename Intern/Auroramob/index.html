About Us

Intro
Welcome to Auroramob! We are a high-tech company focused on developing tool-based applications, dedicated to providing efficient and intelligent digital solutions for users worldwide. Our mission is to enhance the quality of daily life and work efficiency through technological innovation.
Products
Auroramob's core products cover three main areas: short videos, translation, and cameras. Each application is meticulously designed to provide users with the best possible experience.
Establishment
Founded in May 2017, it is located in the beautiful spring city of Jinan, a high-tech enterprise in Shandong Province. It is also one of Shandong's top mobile Internet and artificial intelligence companies.
Members
All the company's founding members graduated from 985 and other double first-class universities in China. In the early stage, the core members worked in Baidu, Innovation Workshop, FunPlus, and other world-class Internet and game companies.
Partnerships
The company signed a strategic cooperation agreement with Tencent, Baidu, ByteDance, and KuaiShou in the domestic market. In overseas markets, the company has reached long-term and important strategic cooperation agreements with Amazon, Google, Apple, Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok.
